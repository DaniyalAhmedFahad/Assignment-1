Peshawari Mutton Karahi

Our Peshawari Mutton Karahi is a popular Pakistani dish originating from Peshawar, the capital of the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province. It is a flavorful and spicy stew made with marinated mutton or goat meat cooked in a karahi (a type of wok) with a blend of aromatic spices, onions, tomatoes, and chilies.


Chicken White Karahi

Chicken White Karahi is a popular Pakistani and North Indian dish ¹. The recipe is known for its rich and creamy texture, which is made possible by the addition of heavy whipping cream ¹. It does not contain red chili powder, but instead, it uses white and black pepper and green chilies to achieve a subtle balance of spiciness.


Chicken Biriyani

There are several types of Chicken Biryani, including Hyderabadi, Lucknowi, Sindhi, and Awadhi, each with its own distinct flavor and cooking style. The dish is often served with raita (a yogurt and cucumber side dish), salad, and papadum, and is garnished with fresh herbs, nuts, and dried fruits.
